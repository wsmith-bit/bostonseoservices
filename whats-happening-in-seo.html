Frequently Asked Questions

What is the biggest SEO change happening in 2025?

The most significant shift is the expansion of AI-generated answers (Google AI Overviews, Bing Copilot, ChatGPT search) that reduce traditional organic clicks. Optimizing to appear as a cited source within AI answers is now a primary SEO goal.

Are backlinks still important in 2025?

Yes, but the emphasis has shifted from quantity to quality and relevance. AI systems use backlinks as trust signals, and a few high-quality, topically relevant links are more valuable than many low-quality ones.

What is zero-click search?

Zero-click search occurs when a user's query is answered directly on the search results page — through AI Overviews, featured snippets, Knowledge Panels, or local packs — without the user clicking to any website.

How do I optimize for Google AI Overviews?

Focus on: (1) writing clear, direct answers to specific questions, (2) implementing FAQPage and Article schema, (3) building topical authority through comprehensive content clusters, and (4) strengthening E-E-A-T signals like author credentials and citations.
