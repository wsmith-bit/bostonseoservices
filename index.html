AEO vs GEO vs AI-first SEO

These terms get abused. Here are the working definitions we use in implementation.

AI-first SEO

Optimize for classic results and AI answers. The baseline is clarity, retrieval formatting, and trust signals.

AEO

Make your page the best extractable answer to specific questions. Great for snippets and zero-click.

GEO

Improve citation likelihood in generated summaries by building authority and consistent entity signals.

Plain English: AEO is about being the answer. GEO is about being cited while the answer is generated.

Not a fit if

  • • You are only looking for paid ads management.

We do the opposite: clarity, structure, and trust. It compounds slower than hacks, and it lasts longer.

Before we start: clarifying questions

These reduce scope confusion and speed up results. If you can answer these, we can build a focused 90-day plan fast.

Offer and positioning

  • • What is your primary service or product that drives revenue?
  • • What is the highest-margin offer you want to lead with?
  • • What do you want to be known for in one sentence?

Sales and operations

  • • What counts as a qualified lead for you?
  • • What areas do you serve and where do you refuse work?
  • • Do you have capacity limits or seasonal constraints?

Trust and proof

  • • What proof exists that we can cite: reviews, case studies, credentials?
  • • What claims are you not comfortable making publicly?
  • • Who are your top 3 competitors?

Tech and constraints

  • • What CMS is the site on and who controls deployment?
  • • Any compliance constraints: medical, legal, financial, brand rules?
  • • Are there existing pages we must not change?

You do not need perfect answers. We just need enough clarity so AI systems do not misclassify what you do.

Common AI search questions

Prompt-first on purpose. This is how people search now, and how AI systems parse intent.

How does AI decide who to cite?

AI favors sources that are easy to extract, consistent across the web, and low-risk to quote. Structure reduces uncertainty.

Why do some brands rank but never show up in AI answers?

Ranking can happen without extraction-ready answers. Citation often requires definitions, structured blocks, and trust signals.

Does AI visibility replace SEO?

No. Technical SEO and authority still matter. AI visibility is an added layer that rewards unambiguous answers and consistent entities.
