Key Terms & Definitions

SEO, or Search Engine Optimization, refers to the practice of enhancing a website's visibility in search engine results. This process involves optimizing content, keywords, and technical aspects to ensure higher rankings on platforms like Google.

Boston SEO Company is a specialized agency focused on providing customized SEO solutions to businesses in Boston and beyond. These companies use local insights and advanced techniques to enhance digital presence and drive organic traffic.

Local SEO is a subset of SEO targeting local search queries. It involves optimizing a business's online presence to attract more customers from relevant local searches. This is crucial for businesses with physical locations or those targeting a particular geographic area.

Generative Engine Optimization involves using AI-driven tools to improve content creation and optimize for search engines. To learn more, explore our Generative Engine Optimization page.
